Why

Feeding your dog a BARF diet (Bones And Raw Food) can vastly improve your dogs overall physical & mental health. Most big box kibble porducts contain processed ingredients which leads to an increased cancer rate in dogs and can further deteriorate their joint health.

Here are some benefits:

  • Health: A balanced, nutritional diet is critical to maintain good health.
  • Activity Level:Working dogs and service dogs have different caloric requirements than household pets.
  • Age & Size:Nutritional requirements differ between puppies and adults, large breed and small breed dogs.
  • Food Allergies:Some dogs are allergic to specific proteins or ingredients such as eggs, corn, wheat, soy, or milk.
  • TasteNo matter what you think, dog food has to taste and smell good – to your dog.

About Us

With a number of years in the dog sport world we have put in the time to build a knowledge base to provide you with an excellent plan to keep your dog healthy and happy!
